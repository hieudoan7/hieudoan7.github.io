October 29, 2021 One minute read

1. Be of + N

The constructor Be Of + Noun is virtually the same as Be + Adj except it sounds more formal. You can think of it as to be of a certain class or of a certain type. Among the best ~ Of the best Ex: The book is of interest Can I be of any help?

3. It vs That(This)

  • both are pronoun
  • IT: without nuance
  • This/that: with nuance (emphasis), also repalce whole phrase/sentence

4. Progressive Aspect (Continuous Aspect)

  • Emphasize the action last long, in a duration

  • The action is in progress at the time of speak

  • pass progressive: not normally do that, not a routine.
